1. Download MultiMC from multimc.org/#Download
  2. When installing, make sure to set the max allowed ram to something like 6000-8000MB
  3. Go to settings, and then change "Update channel" to development, from stable
  4. Click close and then update, then wait.
  5. Click "Add instance"
  6. Click "FTB" and wait for the list to populate
  7. Search for academy, select it then click ok
  8. Wait for install
  9. Double click academy, and follow the steps to add your minecraft account
  10. After adding the account, double click on academy again. If everything went well, it should eventually launch minecraft
  11. Join the server "mc.awillingham.dev"
