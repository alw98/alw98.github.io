What's my tip of the week?

Learn how to use Git!!! Don't just work off of master, commiting whenever you're done for the day. Instead, make use of CI and any time you want to test incremental changes, commit! More important than commiting often, however, is to commit onto dedicated branches. If you have a new issue you've began work on, create a new branch for it and keep your work related to that issue solely on that branch, until you're ready to merge into master! This makes for easy debugging to figure out where something went wrong allows, you to quickly iterate, and prepares you for working in the real world!
